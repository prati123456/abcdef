What We Do & Why We Do

We Code Convent Foundation collects e-waste and donates it for a meaningful reason. Our mission is to address the growing problem of electronic waste while also benefiting those in need. By responsibly collecting and recycling electronic devices, we aim to reduce environmental impact and promote sustainable practices.

Simultaneously, we donate refurbished electronics to underserved communities, bridging the digital divide and empowering individuals with access to technology. Our work is driven by the belief that by repurposing e-waste, we can make a positive impact on both the environment and the lives of those who receive these valuable resources.
